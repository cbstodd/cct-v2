Our Story

The company is owned and managed by successful entrepreneurs and proven business managers with over 65 years of combined experience. We are dedicated to the furthering success of these businesses, benefitting from unique product offerings and growth potential. Organizations which have benefited from our expertise include the tool and die industry, metal feeding systems, canning, general contracting, specialty contracting and office interiors including:

Ball Manufacturing, Coca-Cola Co., Coors Brewing Co., Global Feeding Systems, Miller Brewing Co., Grupo Modelo, Pepsi Co.

Overview

JMS Technology Holdings, LLC is a privately held investment and management organization. We were established to identify and invest in companies which possess unique product offerings and technologies that conduct business in specialty niche markets. The focus of the business is to identify these companies then help to develop, build, and nurture them into market share leaders. The ultimate intention is to implement a long term investment strategy to maximize operating earnings and to increase potential shareholder value. Firms in which we invest and manage include companies with patented technologies or unique intellectual property whose growth is restricted due to either lack of working capital or management expertise. In return for the investment of monies or business management expertise, JMS Technology Holdings typically assumes an equity position with that firm.
